Erkan Karabulut

Hi! I am a CS (computer stuff) guy, seeking to produce new knowledge as I sit in my chair and gobble up freshly-baked knowledge that I access with a few clicks, despite there is nothing new under the sun. If I get bored, I climb some mountains (see figure 1 on the left for proof).

More formally, I am a PhD student doing research on knowledge modeling and reasoning systems for digital twins. I work with a group of smart people at the INtelligent Data Engineering Lab (INDElab), University of Amsterdam. And my supervisors are Dr. Viktoriya Degeler and Prof. Dr. Paul Groth.

Previously, I have obtained my MSc. degree in Computer Science from the Technical University of Munich, and my BSc. degree in Computer Engineering from the Yildiz Technical University, Istanbul .

I worked as a research assistant at the Bavarian Research Institute for Software-Intensive Systems (fortiss) between 2020-2022. And I worked as a student assistant in the Software Quality R&D Lab that is part of the CE department of my university, between 2017-2019.
